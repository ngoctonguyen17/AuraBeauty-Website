You Ask - Aura Beauty Answers!

About product quality?
All Aura Beauty products undergo strict quality control and have safety certifications. We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality products.
Which skin types are these products suitable for?
Products clearly indicate suitable skin types on the packaging, such as oily, dry, sensitive, or combination skin. You can find detailed information in the product description on our website to choose the right type for your skin.
Is there a return policy if the product doesn't suit me?
We support returns if the product has issues or isn't suitable. Please read our return policy on the website for applicable conditions.
How can I sign up for promotions and latest offers?
You can subscribe to our newsletter via email or create an account on our website to stay updated with our exciting promotional programs.
How can I track my order?
After successfully placing an order, you will receive an email with your order number and detailed instructions to track your delivery status on our website.
