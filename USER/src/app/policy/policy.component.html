Our Policies

Return and Exchange Policy

  • We offer a 30 days return and exchange policy to provide you with the best shopping experience possible.
  • If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, you may return or exchange the item within 30 days from the purchase date, as long as it is in its original condition, unworn, and with all original tags attached. For exchanges, please specify the item you'd like in exchange.
  • Certain products, such as those marked "Final Sale," are not eligible for returns or exchanges.
  • Please allow 5-7 business days for return processing once the item is received.

Shipping Policy

  • All orders are processed within 2-3 business days after purchase. For standard shipping, estimated delivery times are 5-7 business days.
  • Expedited shipping options are available at an additional cost and generally take 2-3 business days. During peak seasons, processing times may be extended by an additional 2-3 days.
  • International shipping is available, and delivery times may vary based on the destination. Please note that delays due to customs are beyond our control.

Privacy Policy

  • Your privacy is very important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring it remains secure.
  • Any information collected during your visit, such as contact details and payment information, will only be used to improve your shopping experience and fulfill your orders.
  • We do not share your data with third parties without consent, except as required by law. To safeguard your information, we implement security measures such as data encryption and limited access for authorized personnel only.

Payment Policy

  • We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express, as well as secure payment options like PayPal and Apple Pay.
  • Payments are processed securely through trusted providers, and we do not store your credit card details. If there are any issues with your payment, please contact our support team immediately.
  • For large orders, we may conduct additional verification to prevent fraud and ensure the security of our customers.

Promotional Policy

  • Our promotions, discounts, and special offers are subject to specific terms and conditions.
  • Each promotion may have its own guidelines, including expiration dates, exclusions, and usage limits.
  • Promotions are generally limited to one per customer and cannot be combined with other offers unless stated otherwise.
  • We reserve the right to modify or cancel any promotion at any time. For assistance with promotional codes, please contact our customer service team.

Customer Service Policy

  • We strive to provide exceptional customer service. Our support team is Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM to assist you with any questions, concerns, or feedback.
  • Whether you need help with an order, a return, or product information, our team is here to ensure a smooth and satisfactory shopping experience. Feel free to contact us by phone, email, or live chat during our business hours, and we will respond as promptly as possible.
